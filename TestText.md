# 关于一位实习老师在山区小学的支教经历

### 一、简体中文版（含英文单词）

我从小就想当一名小学老师。上个月我去了中国的一个偏远山区，在那里的一所小学教书。虽然学校里的学生们每天要走很长的路来上学，但是他们每天还是开开心心地来上学。

这所学校学生不多，全校一共只有五十个学生，被分成了两个班。这里有两名老师，一个负责教**Chinese**和**English**，另一个教**Math**和**Science**。我们每天八点半开始上课，两点半就要下课了——因为一些学生需要回家帮家人做家务，有的要到田里种菜，有的要照顾弟弟妹妹。

学校的设备非常简单，这里没有**computer**，更没有**Internet**。但是我更喜欢在这里工作。我觉得这里的孩子和城市里的学生很不一样。城市里的孩子每天花很多时间玩**smartphone**，这直接影响了他们的**learning**。城市里的孩子对学习似乎也没什么**interest**，**learning attitude**也不太好。所以我比较喜欢在山区小学工作。

这里的孩子们每天都充满**energy**，他们对知识的**curiosity**让我感动。虽然条件艰苦，但他们的**smile**是最好的回报。

### 三、英文版（含中文及法文詞彙）

I have wanted to be a primary school teacher since I was a child. Last month, I went to a remote mountainous area in China to teach at a primary school there. Although the students have to walk a long way to school every day, they still come to school happily every day.

There aren't many students at this school — only fifty in total, divided into two classes. There are two teachers here: one teaches **Chinese** and **English**, and the other teaches **Math** and **Science**. We start class at 8:30 every morning and finish at 2:30 in the afternoon. We finish so early because some students need to go home to help their families with housework — some work in the fields, some look after their younger siblings.

The school's facilities are very basic — no **computer**, no **Internet**. But I actually prefer working here. I feel the children here are very different from students in the city. City kids spend too much time on their **smartphones**, which directly affects their learning. City children don't seem to have much interest in studying either — their **xuéxí tàidù** (学习态度) is not great. So I prefer working in this mountain school.

The children here are full of **energy** every day, and their **curiosity** about knowledge truly moves me. **C'est la vie** — despite the difficult conditions, their smiles are the best reward.
